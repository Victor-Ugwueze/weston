About Us

Our health care staff offers you and your pet(s) prompt, courteous, and compassionate service. We offer a wide range of services to the pet owner: Health maintenance examinations, vaccinations, pet dental care, medicine and surgery, emergency service, laboratory and x-ray, nutritional consultation, boarding and grooming, prescription diets, and flea and tick prevention/Treatment. To meet this goal, we have created a clean modern facility with up-to-date equipment combined with a caring, professional staff. You are encouraged to call our office with any questions concerning your pet(s) or if you need information on a pet that you haven’t yet purchased.

Mission Statement

To provide our patients, clients, and community with the highest quality veterinary medical treatment, friendly and caring service, and personalized care. To provide excellent care to our patients throughout all stages of their lives; to foster long term relationships between clients, patients, and doctors; to promote well-being and preventative care; and to provide a professional, courteous atmosphere for our clients. “I think dogs are the most amazing creatures. They give unconditional love. For me they are the role model for being alive.” – GILDA RADNER

PUPPY AND KITTEN CARE: Welcoming a new pet into your family is exciting! Get your new puppy or kitten off to a great start with a specialized package that includes wellness examination and weigh-ins, diagnostic tests, and more!
INTERNAL MEDICINE: Our doctors diagnose and treat serious and chronic health problems related to your pet’s internal organs. We have special interest in endocrinology, gastroenterology, urology, and cardiology.
IMMUNIZATION AND PARASITE CONTROL: We believe the key to your pet’s health starts with annual wellness exams, vaccinations, and diagnostic screenings. Parasite prevention and control is an important part of keeping your pet healthy and happy!
COMPREHENSIVE PHYSICAL EXAMINATIONS: We recommend having an annual wellness examination.
DENTISTRY: Veterinary medicine extends to more than physical conditions. We can provide your pet with a behavioral health consultation to address fear, stress, destructiveness, biting, and more.
NUTRITIONAL COUNSELING: A complete and balanced diet is one of the most important factors in optimizing your pet’s health and longevity. We provide nutritional counseling and customized weight loss plans tailored to your pet’s needs.
ORTHOPEDICS AND SOFT TISSUE SURGERIES: Whether it’s a routine spay/neuter or an emergency, we are dedicated to providing the best possible care for your pet both before and after surgery. We’re fully equipped to perform both orthopedic (ACL surgery, luxating patella and bone fractures) and…
DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES: Our in-house laboratory allows us to perform routine diagnostic screening within minutes, ensuring your pet receives the most appropriate medical treatment in a timely manner.
PET MEDS: Fully stocked for your convenience, we offer prescription foods, treats, and approved medication refills.
HEALTH CERTIFICATES: Proof of vaccinations, health treatments, and other necessary information necessary for traveling with pets.
MICROCHIPPING: Thousands of pets become lost every day, and microchip identification is the one reliable way to reunite these lost pets with their owners. The procedure is safe, inexpensive, and practically painless.
HOUSE/KERNEL FUMIGATION: In case of a heavy tick, lice , rat, or Cockroach infestation, we do a very thorough fumigation of the clients house.
EUTHANASIA AND CREMATION SERVICES: Our focus is on maintaining comfort and quality of life for our terminally ill and geriatric patients. Our experienced staff will be here for you through your pet’s end-stage.
